The Team

Team BlueRidge currently consists of five members with varying backgrounds and interests. The most exciting part about Team BlueRidge is the opportunity to connect with and learn from people and share knowledge back. With such a wide array of talents, there are few things the team can't tackle.

Abhinav Jhanwar

An open-source enthusiast who likes working on Android and Linux projects. I like working with C++ but I also have knowledge of C and Java. I do Android app development as well as work on custom ROMs. I have worked with Qualcomm and Novathor boards. I basically work on Xperia devices and I like helping and learning.

Kyle Laker

After getting involved with Team BlueRidge on the HTC Wildfire S, I put my skills to use in managing our Gerrit and JIRA installations as well as developing the Paste It Android app. My preferred language is Java and I love mobile/web integration.

Rob Sink

A hobbyist who dabbles with a lot of projects. As much interested in learning and supporting open hardware as an enthusiast for open source software. Got a mobile device with a problem? If I don't have an answer for you, I can put you on the right track.

Simon Sickle

Simon is one of the Team BlueRidge founding members. He owns many devices, including: HTC One M8 (Sprint), HTC One SV (Boost), HTC One V (Virgin USA), HTC One V (GSM), HTC Wildfire S (Virgin USA), and Nexus 7 (2012, WiFi) as well as an iPhone 6S and Apple TV 4. His hobbies include improving and securing the Linux kernel on ARM devices and server administration and cyber security. He also enjoys learning new systems; he is leading the way for Team BlueRidge's push towards the Apple platform and, eventually, the Windows Phone platform as well.
